About

I am a second year student studying Design Engineering MEng at Imperial College London. I have a passion for 3D printing and ‘tinkering’. I have always enjoyed prototyping from building prototype electronics and mocking up designs in CAD. My course at university aligns with my interest of physically understanding how to engineer an optimum product, and my artistic fascination of how they can be aesthetically and intuitively designed with a user in mind.

One of my strengths is communication, and I enjoy solving design problems in a team, and trying to find a creative path to a solution. I particularly enjoy finding solutions for users by finding their needs and combining this with relevant technologies and skills I have learnt on my course.
